A New Earth Conversation is a transformative climate initiative at Clark University


We have, as a planet, entered a time of profound reckoning. Given the gravity and uncertainty of our situation, the work of higher education must change.

A New Earth Conversation

is our response to the challenge of these times. Through a collective process of inquiry, listening and discernment, new kinds of awareness, engagement and effective action become possible.
